Adding a Designated Partner

It is mandatory for every Limited Liability Partnership to have at least two designated partners. Two partners from the company are meant to function as the designated partners. To be a designated partner, they need to hold a Designated Partner Identification Number (DPIN). Additionally, they have to be mentioned in the LLP agreement.

Procedure for Adding a Designated Partner

3 Working Days

If the proposed designated partner does not have Designated Partner Identification Number (DPIN), the applicant should apply for one. If the partner already has a DPIN, the same can be used.

4 Working Days

A resolution needs to be passed, approving the appointment of the designated partner, form 4 needs to be filed for change in partner and form 3 for amendment in LLP Agreement.
